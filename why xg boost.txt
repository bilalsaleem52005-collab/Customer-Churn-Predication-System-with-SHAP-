Three machine learning models were evaluated: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost. Logistic Regression provided a strong baseline due to its simplicity and interpretability. Random Forest captured nonlinear relationships but did not significantly improve performance. XGBoost achieved the best overall performance, particularly in identifying churners (higher recall). Because churn prediction prioritizes detecting at-risk customers rather than maximizing overall accuracy, XGBoost was selected as the final production model.
